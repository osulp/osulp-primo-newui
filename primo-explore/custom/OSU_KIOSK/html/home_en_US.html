Valley Library Kiosk Search

Use this system to quickly search for materials here in the Valley Library.


Need a book?

By default this kiosk will search all materials here in the building. Select "Course Reserve" if you only want to search what's on reserve at the Circulation Desk.


Need electronic resources?

This kiosk will only search our local materials, so it won't show any of our online resources. If you're looking for online resources, please use 1Search at any of our Learning Commons computers, or check a laptop out at the circulation desk.


Special Collections

Curious about the materials in our Special Colletions and Archives? Select the "Special Collections and Archives" scope to search what's available at our 5th floor SCARC Reading Room.

Not finding what you need?

Come to the Information Desk on the 2nd floor for help!

 Check out a laptop and more at the Circulation Desk

Need to borrow a laptop, calculator, headphones, or more? Search above or come to the Circulation Desk on the 2nd floor.
